FAQ

Common Questions.

Do you clean all hat types?+
We clean most caps and hats. Some delicate, vintage, or specialty materials may require an evaluation first.
Can you remove sweat rings?+
In most cases, yes — especially with Deep Clean or Restoration+. Results vary based on fabric, dye, age, and any prior damage.
Will my hat look brand new?+
We aim for the best possible restoration. Some staining or fading can be permanent. We'll always be upfront about what's realistic.
Do you reshape hats?+
Yes — shape restore is included in both the Deep Clean and Restoration+ service tiers.
How does mail-in work?+
Order online, pack your hats in a sturdy box with your name and order number, ship to us, and we ship back with tracking when complete.
What's your turnaround time?+
Standard turnaround is 3–7 business days after arrival. Rush service (48–72 hours) available for +$25.
Do you offer OC pickup?+
Yes — scheduled routes covering Santa Ana, Costa Mesa, Irvine, Huntington Beach, Tustin, Fountain Valley, Orange, Garden Grove, and surrounding areas.
What if my hat is heavily damaged?+
We'll assess and let you know what's realistically possible before we begin. We'll always be honest with you about the outcome.
